Biography

I am currently a Professor (Graduate Supervisor) at School of Computer Science and Technology, Zhoukou Normal University, Zhoukou, China. My current research interests include three-dimensional scene reconstruction and scene semantic understanding.

I received the M.S. degrees in Computer Application Technology from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China, Zhoukou, China, in 2009, and the Ph.D. degree in Computer Application Technology from School of Computere Science and Engineering, Chinese Academy of Sciences, Beijing, China, in 2014. From July 2014 to April 2016, I was also a Postdoctoral fellow (advised by Prof. Xukun Shen) at State Key Laboratory of Virtual Reality Technology and System, Beihang University, Beijing, China.

For more details, please refer to: http://people.ucas.edu.cn/~shibiaoxu
